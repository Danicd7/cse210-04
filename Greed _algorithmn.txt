Greed.
1. Player starts the game by typing python3 rfk in the terminal or by clicking the run on the IDE.
2. The screen opens based on the number of fixed rows.
3. The player types the name and the game starts.
4. The symbols of gems, players and rocks appear.
5. Gems (*) and rocks (o) randomly appear and fall from the top of the screen.
6. If the player moves left or right and touches a gem they earn a point.
7. If the player(#) moves left or right and touches a rock they lose a point.
8. If the player touches a Gem or rock, they are removed from the falling.
9. If the player closes the window, The game continues until the player.
